Welcome to the Logic-Circuit Solver!

This app allows you to quickly solve for the output of logic equations given the logic of the input variables.

Type the logic equation that you are trying to solve in the first box below. Please do not include any spaces between characters. For reference, the AND gate is represented by *, the OR gate by +, and the NOT gate by '. Example: F = A+B+C

In the second box, please list the logic of your variables (either 1 or 0) separated with commas and starting with the logic of A. Example: Logic of Variables = 1,0,0
